About the Band

Who They Are

Truck Stop Gamblers are a hard-driving country rock band out of Granbury, Texas — road-tested, no-frills, and built for the kind of rooms where the beer's cold and the crowd means it. Drawing from the deep well of Texas country, red dirt, and straight-up rock and roll, they deliver a live show that earns repeat bookings. Their latest album The Long Way Back features current radio single "Over You," making rounds on Texas and regional country radio. These are guys who've put in the miles and it shows every night on stage.
